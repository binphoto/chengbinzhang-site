Photography Contest Guidelines

Eligibility
● Participation in this photography contest is free. Each photographer may submit up to 5 images.
● Both amateur and professional photographers aged 18 and above are eligible to enter.

Exhibition Details
● Approximately 45 printed photographs will be displayed in each exhibition. Selected artists will also have the opportunity to showcase their work on a 60-inch, 4K digital screen with optimal color quality.
● Artists whose works are selected for the exhibition and featured on the R&D Gallery website will be required to pay a fee:
      ○ $30 for printed photographs
      ○ $10 for digital screen display
● Payment can be made via debit or credit cards.*
● Only photographers whose works are exhibited will incur a fee. All other participants will not be charged.

Printing and Exhibition Support
● Photographs for the exhibition will be printed free of charge by R&D Gallery. Artists may also choose to send their own printed works if preferred.
● Exhibitions organized by R&D Gallery run for two weeks. All associated costs (including printing on 260 gsm paper, framing, hanging, press releases, invitations, opening night events, and supervision) are covered by the gallery at no additional cost to the artists.

Submission Requirements

Frame Sizes

R&D Gallery uses six standard frame sizes:

1.  Vertical: 43.5 x 53.5 cm (17.1" x 21.1")
2.  Horizontal: 53.5 x 43.5 cm (21.1" x 17.1")
3.  Square: 53.5 x 53.5 cm (21.1" x 21.1")
4.  Square: 46.5 x 46.5 cm (18.3" x 18.3")
5.  Vertical: 27 x 64 cm (10.6" x 25.2")
6.  Panoramic: 64 x 27 cm (25.2" x 10.6")

● All displayed photographs will feature a white mat.

High-Resolution Files
● Selected artists must provide high-resolution files suitable for printing upon request. The file format is at the artist's discretion.

Usage Rights
● Submitted works may be used by the gallery for promotional purposes.
● If there is interest from collectors, R&D Gallery will facilitate contact between the artist and the collector. The gallery does not take any commission from sales.

File Preparation and Submission

File Specifications
● Only digital submissions are accepted.
● Image files must be in RGB mode, JPG format, with the longest side set to 1,920 pixels at 72 dpi.
● File names should follow this format: 　lastname-firstname1.jpg, and so on (e.g., lastname-firstname2.jpg).

Submission Process
1. Send an email with the following details:
　　○ Subject line: Name of the contest you are entering.
　　○ Body of the email: Include your name, country of origin, contest title, and the titles of your photographs along with their corresponding file names.

2. Attach your photographs and send the email to: <rdphotoarts@gmail.com>
　　○ For technical support regarding uploads, entry forms, or payments, contact: <rdphotoarts@gmail.com>
