Exhibitions

Life in the Grasslands
（1-March to 31-March）

Welcome to our ‘Exhibitions’ section. We are thrilled to present our latest series, ‘Life in the Grasslands’, a captivating collection of photographs by the esteemed Mr. Shibata. This exhibition takes you on a visual journey to the vast grasslands of Inner Mongolia, offering a rare glimpse into the daily lives of the local herdsmen. Through the lens of Mr. Shibata, you will experience the raw beauty of the landscape and the rich culture of its inhabitants. We invite you to explore and appreciate the artistry and storytelling in each frame. Enjoy the exhibition!
